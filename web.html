About

The Management Club of the College aims to provide a platform for students to develop their management skills, knowledge, and networking opportunities. The club will serve as a hub for students to engage in various management-related activities, events, and competitions, fostering a sense of community and camaraderie among its members

Vision

The Management Club aims to provide a collaborative environment where students can develop practical management skills, leadership qualities, and professional acumen through experiential learning, interactive sessions, and networking opportunities.

Objectives of the Club

Primary Objectives

  • Knowledge Sharing
  • Professional Development
  • Networking
  • Community Building

Secondary Objectives

  • Case Studies
  • Mentorship
  • Social Responsibility

Long-term Objectives

  • Thought Leadership
  • Collaborations
  • Research and Publications
  • Awards and Recognition
